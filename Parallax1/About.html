Who We Are

Nanagar Comics is a comic book tech company that specializes in projecting a world at its eco-infinite potential. This means our company universe is powered by clean-tech and the wildlife is expressed vividly. Our major purpose is to get parents connected with the wildlife in order for them to teach children the most important aspect in their future which is nature and a clean environment.

The current state and direction of the environment puts future generations in danger. Adults living today have the opportunity to preserve Earth for children, Nanagar Comics has created a method to sufficiently get parents to learn and apply the environmental knowledge we offer. We use our universe as a subliminal massage to get customers optimistic about the future. Conservationists and comic book enthusiasts Comic-book media has recently been promoted as Marvel & DC's Hollywood Movies have been populated. The vision is to have the nature and clean energy vividly expressed across Planet earth through the influence of the Nanagar Universe.We will use a cell phone application to execute, the cell app will have the ability to do many things such as view our comics, give valuable facts and tips and give customers the ability to subscribe to our subscription service. Subscription comes with many benefits that will give our customers a feeling of importance.
